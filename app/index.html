What is Bāzì? (八字)

Bāzì (八字) literally means "Eight Characters." It is one of the oldest forms of Chinese metaphysics, refined during the Tang dynasty (618–907 AD) and still practiced widely across East Asia today.

The system holds that your precise birth moment — year, month, day, and hour — encodes eight Chinese characters that reveal your nature, the elemental forces shaping your life, and the timing of your most important opportunities and challenges.

The core idea: You are not separate from nature. Your birth moment places you in a specific position within the flowing cycle of natural forces — and that position shapes everything.

The Four Pillars (四柱 Sìzhù)

Your birth data maps onto four "pillars." Each pillar has two characters — a Heavenly Stem (天干) on top and an Earthly Branch (地支) below. Four pillars × two characters = eight characters.

Yin & Yang (阴阳 Yīn Yáng)

Every element has two faces — Yang (陽, active, outward) and Yin (陰, receptive, inward). Neither is better. Both are essential forces in the universe and in your chart.

In your chart, each of your 8 characters is either Yin or Yang. The balance shapes whether you tend to lead or support, initiate or complete, speak or listen.

The 60-Year Cycle (六十甲子)

How Fortune is Read (六步推算)

A reading works in layers — from your lifetime blueprint down to what's active right now. Each layer adds precision.

1
Establish the Four Pillars

Convert birth date and time into Year, Month, Day, and Hour pillars using the traditional Chinese lunisolar calendar — not simply the Gregorian date.

2
Identify the Day Master

The Day Stem is you — your elemental core. Is your Day Master strong or weak? Supported by other characters or drained by them?

3
Map Element Balance

Count and weigh all 8 characters by element. Excess and deficiency reveal personality extremes, health tendencies, and the types of years that will challenge or support you.

4
Read the 10-Year Luck Pillars (大运)

Every 10 years your chart enters a new energetic environment. These Luck Pillars determine the major themes and opportunities of each decade of your life.

5
Overlay the Annual Pillar (流年)

Each year brings a new Stem+Branch pair that interacts with your natal chart and current Luck Pillar. This is why some years feel aligned and others feel grinding — it's measurable.

6
Synthesize into a Reading

All layers combine: your strengths, repeating patterns, best windows for major decisions, and how to work with — not against — your elemental nature.

Destiny & Free Will (命运与自由意志)

Bāzì doesn't predict a fixed future. It reveals the energetic terrain of your life — the hills and valleys, the seasons of growth and consolidation.

The metaphor masters use: Bāzì is a weather forecast, not a prison sentence. A forecast tells you it will rain — you still choose whether to carry an umbrella, dance in it, or stay inside.

Understanding your chart doesn't limit you. It shows where your natural current flows strongest — so you can swim with it instead of exhausting yourself fighting against it.

The Chinese tradition holds that 命 (fate) sets the stage, but 运 (timing & effort) and 风水 (environment) shape the outcome. Your chart is one-third of the story.
